# v4.6.2 — Design System: Home, About & Vision Pages (2026-05-31)

## What changed

Continues the design-system rollout onto the remaining public content pages.

- **Home (`welcome.blade.php`)**: feature cards → `<x-ui.card>`; "View All Events" and
  "More News" CTAs → `<x-ui.button>`. (The compact event/news cards are kept as the
  intentional home layout; their colors already match the tokens.)
- **About (`about.blade.php`)**: the four "Why YEMDAT" cards → `<x-ui.card>`.
- **Vision (`vision.blade.php`)**: Vision/Mission cards and the five objective cards →
  `<x-ui.card>`. **Fixes a latent bug**: these cards used `bg-yemdat-offwhite`, a color that
  was never defined in the theme, so the cards had no background (nearly invisible). They now
  render as proper white surfaces.

## Files changed (deploy to production)
- `resources/views/welcome.blade.php`
- `resources/views/about.blade.php`
- `resources/views/vision.blade.php`

## Server deployment
1. Upload the three files above.
2. **Rebuild assets**: `npm run build`, then upload `public/build/`.
3. `php artisan optimize:clear`. No migrations needed.

## Verify
- `/`, `/about`, `/vision` render with consistent cards/buttons in LTR + RTL; the Vision/Mission
  and objective cards now have visible white backgrounds.
