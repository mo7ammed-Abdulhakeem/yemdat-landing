# v4.7.4 — Filament Admin: Settings Page + Analytics Widget (2026-05-31)

## What changed

Completes the Filament admin's functional parity with `/admincpanel`.

- **Settings page** (`/admin/manage-settings`): a Filament custom page over the `settings`
  key/value table — Notification Bar, Contact Info, Social Links, and the full bilingual
  "Be a Trainer" form labels (collapsed section). Gated by the `settings` permission.
- **Analytics widget** (`AdminStatsOverview`): dashboard stat cards (Members + 30-day new,
  Events + active, Published Posts, Trainer Requests, Contact Messages, Broadcasts Sent).
  Visible only to admins with the `analytics` permission.

79 tests green (Settings page + widget covered, including permission gating).

> **`/admincpanel` is intentionally NOT removed.** The Filament panel now has parity, but
> the legacy panel is kept as a working fallback until Filament is verified in production.
> Retiring it (deleting routes/controllers/views) should be a deliberate, separate step
> after you've used `/admin` live.
